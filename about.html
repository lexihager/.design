MY STORY

The most interesting aspect of design for me is the storytelling, because ultimately it's our stories that make us human. I often search for stories in the people and cultures around me, while finding inspiration in library books and current events. I grew up in Albuquerque New Mexico, so I am obsessed with green chile and the balloon fiesta is my favorite time of year. I'm kinda weird, but then again I like weird.
