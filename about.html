MY STORY

The most interesting aspect of design for me, is the storytelling. It is what makes us human aferall. I often search for inspiration in new and interesting cultures. I grew up in Albuquerque New Mexico, so I am obsessed with green chile and turquoise feels like home. It’s easy to take design too seriously, so I won’t miss an opportunity to add humor to my work. I'm kinda weird, but then again I like weird.
